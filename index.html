Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable

The city of Swanport was known for its towering, unsecured buildings.

One day, out of nowhere, an earthquake struck. The citizens were terrified, as there was no plan in place for such a disaster.

The citizens banded together and shared what they had, but their rations wouldn't last long.

Thankfully, the Red Cross arrived, providing food and temporary shelter.

Through this experience, the citizens of Swanport learned a valuable lesson about the importance of disaster risk reduction and sustainability.

The end !
